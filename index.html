    <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

    <p><strong>Why does speed show 0 when stationary?</strong><br />

    iDash uses GPS to measure speed. GPS speed readings require movement — this is normal behaviour.</p>

    <p><strong>How do I export a trip?</strong><br />

    Open the Trips list from the menu, tap a trip, then tap Export. Choose GPX for mapping apps or CSV for spreadsheets.</p>

    <p><strong>How do I change the theme?</strong><br />

    Swipe left or right on the dashboard to cycle through themes.</p>

    <p><strong>Can I use iDash without a data connection?</strong><br />

    Yes — all gauges use on-device GPS. An internet connection is only needed for the map tiles and navigation search.</p>
